Bugatti

Bugatti is a French luxury brand known for producing some of the world's fastest and most exclusive cars. Founded in 1909 by Ettore Bugatti, the brand became famous for its performance and iconic designs.
Founded: 1909, Molsheim, France
Founder: Ettore Bugatti
Owner: Volkswagen Group

Bugatti's Top 3 Icons of Speed and Luxury:

Bugatti La Voiture Noire

This one-of-a-kind hypercar is valued at $18 million, featuring an 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine.

Bugatti Centodieci

A limited edition model priced at around $9 million, inspired by the iconic EB110 and powered by a 1600-horsepower engine.

Bugatti Chiron Super Sport 300+

This record-breaking car surpassed 300 mph, making it one of the fastest cars globally, priced around $5.2 million.
