Ferrari

Ferrari is an Italian luxury sports car manufacturer, renowned for its dedication to speed, design, and motorsport heritage. Founded by Enzo Ferrari, the brand has become synonymous with performance and elegance.
Founded: 1939, Modena, Italy
Founder: Enzo Ferrari
Headquarters: Maranello, Italy

Ferrari's Iconic Models:

Ferrari LaFerrari

Ferrari's first hybrid supercar, valued around $1.5 million, equipped with a V12 engine paired with an electric motor to deliver over 950 horsepower, combining power and efficiency in a unique package.

Ferrari 812 Superfast

With a 6.5-liter V12 engine producing 789 horsepower, the 812 Superfast is a front-engine powerhouse, valued at around $330,000, embodying Ferrari’s tradition of high-performance GT cars.

Ferrari SF90 Stradale

Ferrari’s plug-in hybrid supercar with a combined output of nearly 1,000 horsepower, valued at approximately $625,000. The SF90 Stradale is a leap forward in Ferrari’s electrification journey.
