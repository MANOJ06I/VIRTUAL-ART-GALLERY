Experience the diverse world of painted art. Explore our curated collection from contemporary and classic artists.

Leonardo da Vinci

An Italian Renaissance painter who created Mona Lisa, The Last Supper, and Vitruvian Man. This artist is known for vibrant landscapes and abstract themes.less than 20 surviving paintings that can be definitely attributed to him

Pablo Picasso

A Spanish artist who co-founded Cubism and invented collage. His work includes Les Demoiselles d'Avignon and Three Women at the Spring. Known for unique color palettes and expressive brushwork.

Claude Monet

A painter who captured the changing effects of light on landscapes. This artist is known for vibrant landscapes and abstract themes.

Michelangelo

An Italian painter, sculptor, architect, and poet who designed the ceiling of the Sistine Chapel and created Pieta.. Known for unique color palettes and expressive brushwork.

Vincent van Gogh

A Dutch Post-Impressionist painter who is among the most famous and influential figures in the history of Western art. In just over a decade, he created approximately 2100 artworks, including around 860 oil paintings, most of them in the last two years of his life. .
