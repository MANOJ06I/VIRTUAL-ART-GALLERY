Eiffel Tower

Experience the romantic allure of Paris with a visit to the Eiffel Tower, a true symbol of France and one of the world’s most iconic structures.

Great Wall of China

Marvel at the Great Wall, an ancient wonder stretching across China, representing the nation’s resilience and breathtaking landscapes.

Machu Picchu

Discover the mystical ruins of Machu Picchu, nestled in the Andes Mountains, and experience the legacy of the Incan Empire.

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, a testament to love and architectural beauty, stands as one of the most breathtaking landmarks in the world.

Statue of Liberty

Embodying freedom and hope, the Statue of Liberty proudly welcomes visitors to the shores of New York City and the United States.

Bali

Bali is a province of Indonesia and the westernmost of the Lesser Sunda Islands.An exotic tropical destination that flaunts scenic beaches and mountains,

Bagan

Bagan is an ancient city and a UNESCO World Heritage Site in the Mandalay Region of Myanmar.Bagan is famous for its historical monuments and houses around 2000 monuments from different periods

Italy

Italy is well-known for its architecture, culture, art, opera, literature, film, and fashion. This is one of the best countries to go on a foreign tour.

Goa

The state of Goa, in India, is famous for its beaches and places of worship. Tourism is its primary industry, and is generally focused on the coastal areas of Goa, with decreased tourist activity inland.

Kyoto,japan

Kyoto is home to 17 UNESCO World Cultural Heritage Sites and over 2,000 temples and shrines. Additionally, Kyoto was rated “The World's Best City” for 2 consecutive years in 2014 and 2015 by readers of “Travel+Leisure”, one of the world's most influential travel magazines.

salzburg,austria

Salzburg is the city where W. A. Mozart was born and the shooting location for "The Sound of Music". the city of Salzburg is just as beautiful in the summer as it is when buried under a blanket of white snow.

banff,alberta

The natural beauty of Banff's surrounding lakes and mountains made it Canada's first National Park and the world's third national park refuge, and part of the Canadian Rocky Mountain Parks UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Krabi Province,Thailand

Krabi is famous for its impressive karst scenery and breathtaking white sand beaches, where you can enjoy a variety of water sports and outdoor activities. It is also known for numerous marine parks and rainforests with abundant wildlife, mangroves, caves, and waterfalls.

egypt

Egypt is very famous for its ancient civilization and the monuments of the majestic pharaohs, such as the Great Pyramids of Giza, the Great Sphinx, the Egyptian Museum, the GEM (The Grand Egyptian Museum), Sakkara, and Dahshur.
