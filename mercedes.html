Mercedes-Maybach

Mercedes-Maybach is a symbol of luxury and refinement within the Mercedes-Benz family, offering ultra-premium vehicles that combine advanced technology with exceptional craftsmanship.
Founded: 1909 (Maybach), integrated into Mercedes-Benz in the 1960s
Headquarters: Stuttgart, Germany
Parent Company: Mercedes-Benz Group AG

Mercedes-Maybach's Top Luxury Icons:

Mercedes-Maybach S-Class

The flagship sedan valued at around $200,000, featuring a V12 engine and the pinnacle of luxury interiors with customizable configurations.

Mercedes-Maybach GLS 600

This luxurious SUV is valued at approximately $160,000, combining off-road capability with high-end luxury, including reclining rear seats and a champagne cooler.

Mercedes-Maybach S 680

An upgraded version of the S-Class, valued around $230,000, with a twin-turbo V12 engine, unmatched comfort, and exclusive finishes for a personalized experience.
