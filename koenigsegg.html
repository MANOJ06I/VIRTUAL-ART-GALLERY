About Koenigsegg

Koenigsegg is a Swedish manufacturer of high-performance sports cars known for its groundbreaking technology and record-breaking hypercars. Founded in 1994 by Christian von Koenigsegg, the company is a symbol of innovation, speed, and engineering excellence.
Founded: 1994, Ängelholm, Sweden
Founder: Christian von Koenigsegg
Headquarters: Ängelholm, Sweden

Iconic Koenigsegg Models

Koenigsegg Jesko

A hypercar capable of speeds over 300 mph, the Jesko features a 5.0L twin-turbo V8 engine, advanced aerodynamics, and cutting-edge technology. Valued at around $3 million.

Koenigsegg Gemera

The Gemera, a revolutionary 4-seater hypercar, is priced at about $1.7 million. It combines a hybrid powertrain with Koenigsegg's unparalleled performance and innovation.

Koenigsegg Regera

With a price of $2.1 million, the Regera is powered by a twin-turbo V8 paired with three electric motors, delivering 1500 horsepower and a unique single-gear direct drive.
